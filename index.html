Parkinson's Disease - Finger Tapping Test

Finger-tapping test to assess for rapid alternating movement tasks. This task tests your rapid alternating finger movements between your left, right and both hands. Results will be sent back to the treating team.

Instructions

  • Sit comfortably with the keyboard directly in front of you.
  • Before each stage, read the on-screen instruction and watch the short key animation.
  • When the countdown reaches zero, alternate only the two shown keys as quickly as possible.
  • Do not repeatedly strike the same key — alternation errors are counted.
  • Complete all three stages to generate the final report.
